Project 1

I decided to go with the stamp template for my project, using images of minecraft horses for the stamps. Considering how the images were mostly square, I thought having a minecraft theme to the work would be fitting
Press the '1' or '2' key to toggle between the two minecraft horses provided. The 'WASD' keys will move the individual stamps across the canvas. To erase a square, press 'B' then proceed to erase the next square.
I originally was going to include a very amateur sketch of a horse I drew from memory, but the dimensions of the image didn't fit, so I decided to use images of minecraft horses instead as they were readily available.
